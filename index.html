About this document

In this functional overview document we will explain the different functions of the Boozang test tool. This document will be updated as the Boozang test service evolves and an updated copy can always be found on GitHub:

https://ljunggren.github.io/boozang-fo/

The Lab

For an introduction we recommend you check out The Lab at http://thelab.boozang.com. Here you will find videos describing solution to common test automation problems. This document is meant to work more as a reference, but can be read from start to finish as well.

In-tool help

Also, remember that you will find the most essential and up-to-date documentation within the tool. Simple click on the question mark in the toolbar, and click on any function you are interested in. A brief explanation of the function will be annotated directly in the tool.

Contact us

Also, for any issues or questions, email us at administrator@boozang.com or reach out on our Slack channel boozang.slack.com.
